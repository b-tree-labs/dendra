# Dendra Trademark Policy

**DENDRA**, **TRANSITION CURVES**, and **AXIOM LABS** are
trademarks of B-Tree Ventures, LLC. This document describes what
uses of those marks are permitted without advance permission
("fair use") and what uses require a commercial trademark license.

Neither the Apache 2.0 license nor the Business Source License
1.1 that govern Dendra's source code grants any right to use
these marks. Trademark rights are separate from copyright and
patent rights.

## Fair use — no permission needed

You may use the DENDRA name and the `@ml_switch` decorator name
in the following ways without contacting us:

- **Describing your project's use of Dendra.** Example: "Our
  ticket triage service uses Dendra for graduated rule-to-ML
  migration." Naming the library as a dependency is descriptive
  use (nominative fair use).
- **Citing the paper or the benchmarks in academic or
  educational contexts.** No license required for scholarly
  citation.
- **Writing blog posts, tutorials, or talks about Dendra.**
  Use the name as a noun ("Dendra supports X") without treating
  the mark as generic ("our dendra"). Avoid suggesting official
  endorsement.
- **Interoperability statements.** "Compatible with Dendra
  v0.2+" is fine. "Dendra Certified" or "Powered by Dendra"
  requires a license.
- **Bug reports, support questions, and contributions.**
  Referring to the project by name is always fine.

## Uses that require a commercial trademark license

Contact `trademarks@b-treeventures.com` before:

- **Selling goods or services under a name incorporating
  "Dendra" or a confusingly similar variant.**
- **Running a website, SaaS, or hosted offering titled "Dendra
  Something" or "Something Dendra".**
- **Producing merchandise** (T-shirts, stickers, mugs) featuring
  the DENDRA word mark or logo for sale.
- **Representing your product as "Dendra Certified",
  "Dendra Approved", "Official Dendra Partner", or any variant
  implying quality assurance or partnership.**
- **Registering domains, social-media handles, or package names
  that begin with "dendra-" for commercial use.** Non-commercial
  fan sites and descriptive community handles are fine.

## The `@ml_switch` decorator and API names

Dendra's decorator identifier (`@ml_switch`) and other public
API names are part of the library's source, which ships under
Apache 2.0 (client SDK) or BSL 1.1 (operated components).
Copying those identifiers as part of normal library use
(importing, wrapping, extending) is covered by the license, not
by trademark.

If your fork re-exports `@ml_switch` with different semantics in
a way likely to confuse Dendra users, contact us first —
trademark law covers naming confusion even where copyright does
not.

## Logo and visual identity

No Dendra logo has been finalized as of this writing. When one
is published, its use will be governed by the same rules as the
word mark: fair use in descriptive references is fine; use
implying endorsement or partnership requires a license.

## Reporting misuse

If you see the DENDRA name or an Axiom Labs brand used in a way
that seems to imply endorsement or that could confuse users,
please email `trademarks@b-treeventures.com`. Low-severity
misuse is usually resolved with a polite note asking for
updated wording.
