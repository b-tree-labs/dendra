# Code of Conduct

Behavior expected of contributors:

- **Be kind.** Treat others with dignity. Disagreement is fine;
  personal attacks are not.
- **Be specific.** Feedback on code, behavior, or decisions is
  more useful when concrete.
- **Be professional.** Keep conversation focused on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, sustained
disruption, and doxxing. Reports can be sent to
`ben@b-treeventures.com`. The maintainer will review and respond
within 5 business days.

Consequences for violations range from a warning to permanent
ban from the project, at the maintainer's discretion, scaled to
severity.
