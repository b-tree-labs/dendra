# Getting help with Dendra

Thanks for using Dendra. This page lists how to get help depending
on what you need.

## Bugs, feature requests, questions

Open an issue on GitHub: https://github.com/b-tree-labs/dendra/issues.

Pick the template that matches:

- **Bug report** — something doesn't work as documented.
- **Feature request** — propose a new capability. For
  patent-adjacent or architecture-level proposals, please check
  `CONTRIBUTING.md` §Philosophy first.
- **Question** — usage questions that aren't bugs. Answers often
  become FAQ entries.

We try to acknowledge new issues within 2 business days.

## Security disclosures

**Do not file security issues publicly.** See
[`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md) for the private disclosure
channel. In short: email `security@b-treeventures.com` with
reproduction steps, and we'll coordinate a fix + public
disclosure timeline with you.

## Commercial licensing

If you want to use Dendra's BSL-licensed components (the
analyzer, the ROI reporter, or future hosted surfaces) in a way
that the standard BSL Additional Use Grant doesn't cover —
specifically, if you want to offer a hosted Dendra-derivative
service — you need a commercial license. Email
`licensing@b-treeventures.com` with your use case.

See [`LICENSING.md`](./LICENSING.md) for the plain-English guide
to what the split license covers without needing a commercial
agreement.

## Trademark questions

DENDRA and B-TREE LABS are trademarks (or pending trademarks) of
B-Tree Ventures, LLC. Most uses you'd want are covered by
nominative fair use — see [`TRADEMARKS.md`](./TRADEMARKS.md).
For anything beyond that, email `trademarks@b-treeventures.com`.

## Design-partner program

We're running a small invite-only design-partner program for the
first 3-5 companies integrating Dendra into production
classifiers. Partners get direct founder Slack access, input on
the roadmap, and priority access to Dendra Cloud when it ships.
Contact `partners@b-treeventures.com` with (a) the classifier
you're thinking about wrapping, (b) approximate monthly volume,
and (c) your timeline.

## Community

- **GitHub Discussions:** https://github.com/b-tree-labs/dendra/discussions
  — for longer-form conversations (design proposals, pattern
  sharing, war stories) that don't fit as issues.
- **Social:** follow `@btreelabs` on X / Bluesky for release
  notes and launch updates.

## Paid support

Paid support plans (priority response SLAs, dedicated Slack
channel, on-call assistance) will be offered with the Cloud
Team / Pro / Scale tiers when Dendra Cloud ships. Until then,
commercial-support engagements are handled ad hoc — email
`hello@b-treeventures.com`.
