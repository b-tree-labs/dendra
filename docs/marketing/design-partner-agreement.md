# Design Partner Agreement — Dendra

**Template. Not legal advice.** Have an attorney review before
first use. This is a founder-authored, plain-language contract
intended to close small-dollar deals ($10-25k) fast without a
Salesforce-style negotiation dance. For Enterprise deals (>$50k)
use a full MSA with an attorney.

---

**"Effective Date"**: the date last signed below.

## 1. What Vendor provides

During the six (6) month term beginning on the Effective Date,
Vendor will provide the Partner:

1. **Priority access to Dendra Cloud** upon its public launch
   (expected approximately 8-12 weeks from Effective Date),
   including unlimited classifications/month usage at no
   additional charge during the design-partner period.
2. **Direct Slack (or equivalent) channel** with the founder for
   technical questions, custom-pattern development, and roadmap
   discussion. Response target: 1 business day.
3. **Integration support** for up to **2 classification sites**
   in Partner's production codebase, using the Apache 2.0
   Dendra library. Support includes: deployment guidance, custom
   AST patterns if Partner's code shape isn't covered by the
   shipped library, and co-design of domain-specific rules or ML
   heads where applicable.
4. **Custom domain pack**: Vendor will produce, at no additional
   charge, one domain-specific ML head + evaluation corpus
   targeted at one of Partner's identified classification sites.
   Scope to be agreed in writing within 30 days of Effective Date.
5. **Roadmap influence**: Partner's feature requests will be
   considered at a priority level above general community input,
   subject to Vendor's sole judgment on product direction.

## 2. What Partner provides

1. **Fee**: **$[15,000 / 20,000 / 25,000]** (pick one) paid
   in full within thirty (30) days of Effective Date. Non-
   refundable except as provided in §6.
2. **Integration access**: Partner will make reasonable efforts
   to integrate Dendra into at least one production
   classification site and operate it for a minimum of 30 days
   during the term.
3. **Feedback**: monthly 30-minute review calls with Vendor's
   founder. Partner may cancel any individual call without
   penalty.
4. **Case-study rights**: Partner grants Vendor a non-exclusive,
   perpetual license to:
   - Publicly identify Partner as a Dendra design partner.
   - Reference measured results (e.g., transition depth, accuracy
     deltas) from Partner's Dendra deployment, in aggregate form,
     in Vendor's marketing materials, paper, and academic
     publications.
   - Co-publish a case study with Partner, with Partner's pre-
     approval of the final text.
   - Partner retains the right to review and approve any specific
     wording before publication. Partner may redact any
     information it deems confidential.

## 3. Intellectual property

1. **Dendra library.** Licensed under Apache 2.0. Partner's
   rights under that license are unchanged by this Agreement.
2. **Custom work.** Any code or models Vendor develops
   specifically for Partner during the term (custom AST patterns,
   custom domain pack from §1.4, custom integration code) is
   owned by Vendor and licensed to Partner under Apache 2.0.
   Partner may use, modify, and redistribute the custom work
   under Apache terms.
3. **Partner-provided data.** Partner retains all rights in any
   data, code, or content it shares with Vendor. Vendor will not
   republish Partner's data in identifiable form without
   Partner's written consent. Aggregate statistics drawn from
   Partner's Dendra deployment (e.g., "design partners observed
   transition depths between N and M outcomes") are permitted.
4. **Vendor patent.** Vendor has filed a provisional patent
   application on the Dendra primitive. This Agreement grants
   Partner a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to
   practice any claims of that patent insofar as Partner is using
   the Apache-2.0-licensed Dendra library or its derivative
   works. Partner does not gain any right to develop competing
   products based on the patented subject matter.

## 4. Confidentiality

1. Each party agrees not to disclose to third parties any
   non-public information received from the other party in
   connection with this Agreement without prior written consent.
2. Obligations survive for three (3) years after the end of the
   term.
3. Information that is publicly available, independently
   developed, or received from a third party without a
   confidentiality obligation is not subject to this section.

## 5. Representations and warranties

1. Vendor represents that, to the best of its knowledge, the
   Dendra library does not infringe any third-party intellectual
   property rights.
2. Vendor makes **no warranty of uninterrupted service** during
   the design-partner period. Dendra Cloud is a developmental
   product during the term; outages, downtime, or data loss are
   possible and are not grounds for additional remedy.
3. Partner accepts the Dendra library and Dendra Cloud **"as
   is"** during the term, except that Vendor will use commercially
   reasonable efforts to respond to reported issues within the
   Slack channel (§1.2) and to remedy reproducible defects.

## 6. Termination

1. **For convenience by Partner**: Partner may terminate this
   Agreement at any time with thirty (30) days' written notice.
   The fee under §2.1 is non-refundable upon payment except that,
   if Partner terminates within thirty (30) days of Effective
   Date for reasons of material non-performance by Vendor,
   Partner is entitled to a pro-rata refund of the unused
   portion.
2. **For convenience by Vendor**: Vendor may terminate this
   Agreement with thirty (30) days' written notice and will
   provide Partner a pro-rata refund of the unused portion of
   the fee.
3. **For material breach**: either party may terminate upon
   thirty (30) days' written notice of a material breach that
   is not cured in that period.

## 7. Post-term

1. Upon expiration of the term, Partner may continue to use the
   Apache-2.0 Dendra library without obligation. Continued use
   of Dendra Cloud after the term is subject to Vendor's then-
   current pricing.
2. Vendor may convert Partner's engagement into a standard
   paid Dendra Cloud subscription at any tier at Partner's
   then-applicable usage level, at Partner's option, no later
   than the date of term expiration.
3. §2.4 (case-study rights), §3 (IP), §4 (confidentiality), and
   this §7 survive termination or expiration.

## 8. Limitation of liability

In no event will either party be liable to the other for
consequential, incidental, or punitive damages. Each party's
aggregate liability under this Agreement will not exceed the
total fees paid by Partner under §2.1.

## 9. General

1. **Governing law**: State of Texas, without regard to conflict-
   of-law provisions.
2. **Dispute resolution**: parties will first attempt a 30-day
   good-faith negotiation. If unresolved, disputes will be
   settled by binding arbitration under American Arbitration
   Association Commercial Rules in Austin, Texas.
3. **Entire agreement**: this Agreement is the complete
   agreement between the parties on the design-partner
   engagement and supersedes prior discussions. Amendments
   require a writing signed by both parties.
4. **Assignment**: neither party may assign this Agreement
   without the other's written consent, except to a successor
   in interest as part of a merger, reorganization, or sale
   of substantially all assets.
5. **Notices**: email to the signatories below is sufficient.
